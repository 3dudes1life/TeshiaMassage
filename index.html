My Journey

My journey to becoming a holistic health practitioner is deeply rooted in family, faith, and lived experience.

In 2004, I experienced three CVAs (strokes) affecting the cerebellum of my brain. This resulted in right-sided weakness, slurred speech, and a blood clot. During that time, I struggled significantly with prescription medications and ultimately chose to pursue natural healing.

Inspired by my grandfather, Remus Townend, I committed fully to holistic health and enrolled at Healing Hands Holistic Health School in Escondido, California, located on Mission Avenue.

Today, I feel clear, grounded, and deeply grateful. My experiences have shaped the compassionate, intentional care I now offer others.
